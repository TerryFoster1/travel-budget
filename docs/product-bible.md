# Executive Summary

Travel Budget is a travel membership platform that helps people gradually accumulate travel purchasing power through recurring contributions.

Travel Budget is NOT:

- A bank
- A savings account
- A budgeting app
- A travel agency
- A financing company
- A Buy Now Pay Later service

Travel Budget is a hybrid between:

- A travel membership
- A travel wallet
- A travel marketplace
- A travel opportunity engine

The fundamental insight behind Travel Budget is:

Most families can afford a vacation over the course of a year.

Most families cannot afford a vacation all at once.

The second insight is:

Even when families try to save, life gets in the way.

The car breaks.

The furnace dies.

Christmas arrives.

The vacation money disappears.

Travel Budget solves this by converting vacation savings into dedicated travel purchasing power.

Users contribute regularly and build a Travel Balance.

As their balance grows, travel opportunities become available.

Users can spend their balance on trips that match their preferences whenever they choose.

# The Problem

Current travel purchasing requires:

1. Choose destination.
2. Choose dates.
3. Determine total cost.
4. Save money.
5. Hope nothing interrupts the savings process.

This process fails for many people.

Travel Budget reverses the order:

1. Decide you want to travel.
2. Begin contributing.
3. Build travel purchasing power.
4. Discover opportunities.
5. Book when ready.

---

# Core Product Definition

Travel Budget allows users to build a Travel Balance through recurring contributions.

Travel Balance represents purchasing power within the Travel Budget ecosystem.

Travel Balance is:

- Dedicated to travel.
- Used toward eligible travel opportunities.
- Flexible regarding destination.

Travel Balance is NOT:

- A traditional savings account.
- An investment account.
- A line of credit.
- A loan.
